## Pricing Strategies

### Individual Controls
- **Basic**: $49-99 per control
- **Advanced**: $149-299 per control
- **Enterprise**: $499+ per control

### Bundles
- **Starter Pack**: 3-5 controls for $199-399
- **Professional Pack**: 10-15 controls for $599-999
- **Enterprise Bundle**: All controls for $1,999-4,999

### Licensing Models
- **Per Organization**: One-time fee per organization
- **Per Environment**: Fee per Power Platform environment
- **Subscription**: Annual or monthly licensing
- **Perpetual**: One-time purchase with optional support/updates
